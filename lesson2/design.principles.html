Visual Hiearchy

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

TheChurchofJesusChrist.org

I chose the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for Visual Hiearchy becuase they do a really good job of putting the most important things first. For example the first thing you see on the page is the most recent and more important, right now it's a message from the prophet.

Hicks Law

Idaho Central Credit Union

iccu.com

Idaho Central Credit Union is a good example of Hicks law. When you first go to the webpage you only have about four options. Hicks Law states that the fewer options the better. They were able to get lots of options down to just a couple really important ones.

Contrast

Heart of Travel

heartoftravel.org

Heart of Travel does a good job of contrast with their background. They have a bunch of different pictures that draw your eye and emphasize their product. It's very effective.
