Noto Sans JP

I chose Noto Sans JP as my heading font because it is a little more robust. It looks professional but It’s also classy. I think it looks hardy enough to be the fond for an adventure webpage.

Lato

Lato is thinner and is less pronounced than Noto Sans JP. That’s why I chose it for the rest of my page. I think it goes will with my heading font and it looks nice and clean.
