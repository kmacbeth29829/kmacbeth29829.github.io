Temple Inn and Suites Site Plan

Site Purpose

Originally we had the idea for this
business from some of our best friends.They work in Temples for the Church of
Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and travel to different temples frequently.
One of the biggest struggles they have with this is trying to find a nice place
to stay. On some trips they need a place to stay for months, while others just a
few days. We strive to provide nice family friendly places to stay for anyone.

Color Scheme

Typography

Open Sans is a very professional font that looks clean and modern and it will be
used for our headings. Quicksand is a little thinner and will be used for our paragraphs.

Site Map

Target Audience

Temple Inn and Suites is a place for everyone.They don't have to be going to a
temple or working in a temple to stay with us. We
strive to have all our destinations radiate a feeling of peace and comfort,
they are great places to stay regardless of your religion.
